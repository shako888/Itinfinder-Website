About ItinFinder

Where wanderlust meets intelligence.

Our Story

ItinFinder was born from a simple frustration: travel planning takes way too long. We built an AI companion that crafts personalized day-by-day journeys in seconds, not hours.

Whether you're a solo backpacker or a family of five, our mission is to make exploration effortless and inspiring. No more endless tabs or outdated guidebooks.
